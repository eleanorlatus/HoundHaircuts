Our Services

See below for a list of our services and prices.

Full groom

Our full groom is suitable for both long and short haired dogs. Following an initial consultation, I will bath, towel and blow dry your dog then give them a full body styling and finish the groom off with a spritz of doggy aftershave.

Prices:
Small breed: £25
Medium breed: £35
Large breed: £40

Bath & brush

Our bath & brush is perfect for dogs that are in-between grooms or pups that enjoy rolling in the mud! I will freshen your pup up by giving them a bath, full brush down followed by a towel and blow dry.

Prices:
Small breed: £15
Medium breed: £25
Large breed: £30

Pawdicure

Help keep on top of your dogs nail length by booking them in for a pawdicure! I will trim their nails and tidy up their paw fur. I also offer a treatment plan for dogs with overgrown nails, please contact me for more information.

Price:
£5

Ultrasonic teeth brushing

Oral hygiene in dogs is so important and proper management with regular brushing can help avoid costly dental treatment in the future. I offer silent teeth brushing using an ultrasonic toothbrush to remove plaque. This is perfect for nervous dogs.

Prices:
30 minute session: £40
Add on to a grooming package: £25
5x30 minute sessions: £175

Puppy introduction

Get your puppy used to sounds and smells of the salon. Come have a chat over a cuppa and we can discuss the best way forward for your puppy.

Price:
From £10

Pick up and drop off

For an extra fee, we are able to collect your dog and drop them off back home after their groom. Prices depending on location and times, call us to find out more.
